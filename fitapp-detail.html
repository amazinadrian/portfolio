FIT Tracker App

React, Mongoose, MongoDB, Firebase

After learning React, I wanted to create an app that people are excited to use. That is where FIT Tracker came along. FIT Tracker is a fitness app that is able to keep track of workouts and calories burned on a monthly to yearly basis. This was a fun project where I was able to make a brand new database from scratch using MongoDB. Everything was visually appealing with your very own fitness chart to see where you've been and where you're going in terms of your fitness goals. Im excited to show you FIT Tracker and I hope you get fit with me!
